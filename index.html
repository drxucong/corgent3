Fixture designs built for the realities of the production floor为真实产线而生的治具设计

Every design is mechanically modelled and simulated before fabrication — so what gets built lands ready to run at full takt time.每一套设计在制造前都经过机械建模与仿真，使最终成品到线即可满节拍运行。

  • Sub-50 µm contact targets亚 50 µm 接触精度目标

    Precision tooling-plate and guided-probe design for consistent contact across millions of cycles.精密定位板与导向探针设计，在数百万次循环中保持稳定接触。

  • Fast changeover & modularity快速换型与模块化

    Tool-less swap concepts and standardised interfaces designed to cut line downtime between product variants.免工具更换概念与标准化接口设计，缩短产品换型停机时间。

  • Operator-safe & ergonomic人因安全与工效设计

    Designed around IEC safety practice with poka-yoke loading and clear pass/fail feedback.遵循 IEC 安全实践，配备防呆上料与清晰的良/不良判定反馈。

How we work工作流程

A disciplined path from concept to handover从概念到交付的严谨路径

A transparent, milestone-driven process keeps your program on schedule and audit-ready.透明、以里程碑驱动的流程，让项目按期推进并随时可审计。

STEP 01

Discover & DFT review需求梳理与 DFT 评审

We analyse your product, test requirements and volume targets, then define a coverage and architecture plan.分析产品、测试需求与产能目标，制定覆盖率与架构方案。

STEP 02

Design & simulate设计与仿真

Mechanical, electrical and software design proceed in parallel, validated through simulation before any metal is cut.机械、电气与软件并行设计，并在加工前通过仿真验证。

STEP 03

Prototype & validate样机与验证

We support prototype build and run correlation studies against golden samples to prove the design is repeatable.支持样机制作并对标金样进行相关性研究，验证设计的重复性。

STEP 04

Handover & support交付与支持

Design handover, engineering documentation and on-site support for a smooth production ramp.提供设计交付、工程文档与现场支持，助力产线平稳爬坡。
